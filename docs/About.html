About

Originally from Queens, New York, studied at two different high schools. She studied at, formerly known as Queens Vocational and Technical High School, now Queens Technical High School. She majored in graphic design before transferring to a career and technical school, Art and Design High School, where she completed the remaining 3 years there as a graphic design major. She received her Photosop CS6 certificate at A&D and interned through a CTE program where she received opportunities to intern for a couple of companies/industries. After graduating, she attended the City College of New York (CUNY) as a undergraduate student majoring in Electronic Design and Multimedia for 4 semesters before transferring to Penn State. She chose to tranfer to Penn State because she knew her career opportunities would broaden with PSU. After taking a chance in applying, she got accepted and is currently finishing the remaining time at Penn State Behrend pursuing DIGIT
