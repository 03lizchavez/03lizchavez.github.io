About

Originally from Queens, New York, I studied at two different high schools. I studied at, formerly known as Queens Vocational and Technical High School, now Queens Technical High School for a year. I majored in graphic design before transferring to a career and technical school, Art and Design High School, where I completed the remaining 3 years as a graphic design major. I received a Photoshop CS6 certificate at A&D and interned through a CTE program where I received opportunities to intern for a couple of companies/industries. After graduating, I attended the City College of New York (CUNY) as an undergraduate student majoring in Electronic Design and Multimedia for 4 semesters before transferring to Penn State. I transferred to Penn State because I knew opportunities would broaden with PSU. After taking a chance to apply, I got accepted and am currently finishing the remaining time at Penn State Behrend pursuing DIGIT. DIGIT is short for Digital Media, Arts, and Technology.
